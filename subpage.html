Recipe of Sixpence

Ingredients

  • pocketfull Rye
  • quarter cup Four and Twenty
  • Frozen Pie Crust
  • pinch of birdsong
  • Blackbirds, one flock
  • Dash of Dainty
  • 1lb King

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 375F
  2. Defrost Rye
  3. Add in Four and Twenty
  4. Cover with Pie Top
  5. Brush Pie top with Egg wash
  6. Bake for 45 min
  7. Remove from oven and Let cool

Helpful Tips

  • Use Younger Blackbirds
  • Use a sweet crust for a dessert pie. In this version, use fatter blackbirds
  • Seek legal aid before presenting to the King

Give the pie to someone else before the birds get out. This shouldn't be your problem.
